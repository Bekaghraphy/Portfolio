WHO AM I?
I am a visual storyteller crafting cinematic narratives with documentary sensitivity. From concept to final cut, I focus on emotion, rhythm, and meaning.
Origins
I was born in a small room beneath a staircase, in an alley within an alley. There, I learned early that life is understood with the heart before the eyes.
Seeing with the Heart
A blind man once recognized me without seeing me. That moment taught me how true vision works.
The Journey
My journey moved through streets, arts, conflicts, and stories. I am still learning. Still moving.
Purpose
Purpose became clear with family. Impact mattered more than success.
Values & Beliefs
Story before technique Meaning over noise Simplicity is strength Consistency over moments
